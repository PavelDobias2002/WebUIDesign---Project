    Description:

    Twin brothers Hal and Bill owned such a toy monkey as kids, and couldn’t help noticing that whenever it was around, parents and friends seemed to perish with alarming regularity, in various hideous ways. Just throw it away, right? Well, the brothers tried that, hacking up the cursed plaything with a cleaver then dropping it down a well. And that seemed to quell the bloodshed for a while. But in adulthood, the murders start back up with a vengeance – and they’re grislier than ever. To protect Hal and Bill’s own families, the stick-wielding simian must be stopped – but how?
